# 📊 Excel Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Capabilities

This repository serves as a professional showcase of my advanced Excel capabilities. I specialize in transforming raw, unstructured datasets into structured analytical models that drive business insights through logical functions, multidimensional pivoting, and dynamic data visualization.

---

## 🛠 Core Technical Proficiencies

### **Advanced Formula Engineering & Logic**
* **Relational Mapping:** Expert use of `VLOOKUP` and `UNIQUE` to perform cross-table data merges and identify distinct attributes within large datasets.
* **Conditional Aggregations:** Implementing complex criteria-based math using `SUMIFS`, `COUNTIFS`, and `AVERAGEIFS` to segment performance by demographics and regions.
* **Logical Classification:** Developing nested logic and threshold-based formulas to automate data categorization (e.g., High/Medium/Low volume tiering).
* **Temporal Analysis:** Utilizing `DATE`, `MONTH`, and `YEAR` functions to decompose timestamps for time-series auditing and seasonal trend analysis.

### **Multidimensional Data Modeling (PivotTables)**
* **Complex Summarization:** Building robust PivotTables to synthesize thousands of rows into actionable summaries, focusing on commissions, quantities, and profitability.
* **Granular Segmentation:** Slicing data by multiple dimensions simultaneously—such as Country, Age Group, and Product Category—to pinpoint specific market drivers.
* **Calculated Fields:** Enhancing standard pivots with custom calculations to derive deeper financial metrics without altering source data.

### **Data Hygiene & Transformation**
* **ETL Fundamentals:** Cleaning and preparing "messy" retail data by standardizing formats, removing duplicates, and resolving errors.
* **Precision Filtering:** Applying advanced sorting and multi-level filtering to isolate specific customer cohorts or high-value product segments.
* **Conditional Formatting:** Using visual logic to highlight outliers, trends, and performance benchmarks directly within the spreadsheet.
